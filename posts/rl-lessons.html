Hard Lessons from Small Reinforcement Learning Runs

Jul 2, 2025 • AI

Constraint: Micro budgets; each experiment must justify itself in < 3 seeds.

Loop: Hypothesis → 3 seeds → variance band check → keep or kill. >5 seeds to show signal = placebo.

Instrumentation: Minimal console dashboard for return Δ, entropy, KL, gradient norm.

logStep({ step, return: epReturn, entropy: policy.entropy(), kl: klDiv });

Outcome: 60% of tweaks killed within a day; earlier detection of collapse ~2k steps sooner.
